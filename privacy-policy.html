01 Introduction

Welcome to Stride Three Studios ("we," "our," or "us"). We are an independent game development studio committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our mobile games and related services.

By downloading, installing, or using any of our games, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our games.

This policy applies to all games published by Stride Three Studios on Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

02 Information We Collect

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our games, we automatically collect certain information through our analytics and advertising SDKs:

  • Device Information: Device type, model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA), and hardware specifications
  • Usage Data: Gameplay sessions, levels completed, in-game actions, session duration, crash reports, and performance metrics
  • Network Data: IP address, mobile network carrier, and connection type (Wi-Fi / cellular)
  • Location Data: General geographic region (country/region level) derived from IP address only — we do not collect precise GPS location

Information from Third-Party SDKs

Our games integrate the following third-party SDKs that may collect data independently under their own privacy policies:

In-App Purchases

If you make in-app purchases, payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not collect, process, or store your payment card information. We only receive a confirmation that a purchase was completed.

06 European Union Users — GDPR

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict or object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
When you first launch our games in the EU/EEA, you will be presented with a consent dialog asking for your permission to show personalized advertisements. You may allow or decline. If you decline, you will still see ads but they will not be personalized based on your interests.

To exercise any of your GDPR rights, please contact us at devs@stridethreestudios.com.

07 Children's Privacy

Our games are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the EU/EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at devs@stridethreestudios.com and we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Our games are rated for general audiences on app stores. We do not knowingly target advertising to children.

08 Data Retention

We retain data collected through our games for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Analytics data is typically retained for 24 months.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at devs@stridethreestudios.com.

09 Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no internet or electronic storage transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

10 Your Advertising Choices

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time using your device settings:

🤖 On Android
  • Go to Settings > Google > Ads
  • Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • Or reset your Advertising ID
🍎 On iOS
  • Go to Settings > Privacy > Tracking
  • Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Or go to Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising and disable "Personalized Ads"

Please note that opting out of personalized ads does not mean you will stop seeing ads in our games. You will continue to see ads but they will be less relevant to your interests.
